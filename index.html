Who are we?

Saran Industries is a private space corporation founded by Rithviksaran who's dedicated to building the future of human space exploration, defense, and interplanetary travel. Our mission is to design and develop advanced aerospace technologies that redefine what is possible from reusable space spacecrafts and heavy-payload launch systems to sustainable interplanetary infrastructure and defense solutions that protect humanity beyond Earth. We believe space is not just the next frontier - it is humanity's destiny. By combining engineering innovation, bold vision, and an unshakable commitment to progress, Saran Industries aims to create technologies that will carry people, cargo, and civilization itself into the stars. This is more than a corporation. This is the beginning of a movement — Glory for Mankind.

01History

July 21st, 2023. A date that marked not just an incident, but a transformation. On this day, in the life of one young visionary, everything changed. A devastating moment that would become the catalyst for something far greater than anyone could have imagined. Four days later, Rithviksaran US founded Garudan Aerospace Research Agency emerged, GARA born from determination and an unwavering belief in what seemed impossible. The first vision was ambitious: Garudan (G9), a spacecraft capable of housing hundreds of people, carrying them beyond Earth's atmosphere toward new frontiers. A mother-ship in every sense. But reality demanded a more practical approach. Resources were limited, and the path to such an undertaking would require strategic steps. The focus shifted to Mission INDRA, an adaptable, multi-role spacecraft designed to revolutionize space transportation. Not just another vehicle, but a platform built to change how we think about reaching space. There was, however, one significant challenge: age. At fifteen, the founder faced skepticism from a world accustomed to credentials and conventional paths. The response varied, some rejected the ideas outright, some questioned the audacity, others simply watched in silence. I suppose we all thought that, one way or another. Yet with each setback came adaptation. Where others saw obstacles, he found opportunities. Where barriers appeared, he discovered workarounds. Each setback became a learning experience, each challenge refined his approach. However, legal restrictions on minors created significant hurdles. Progress was hindered not by technical limitations or lack of vision, but by regulatory constraints that prevented full implementation of the ideas. After two major setbacks, neither due to engineering failures but simply because of age-related legal barriers, a decision was made to take a different approach. The pivot was decisive: self-directed learning in Aerospace Engineering. With limited financial resources but unlimited access to free educational materials, the foundation began, rebuilding from the ground up. Knowledge became the new currency, and determination the driving force. Work commenced on the Indra's S8 engine to develop a minimum viable product. But the demanding schedule of CBSE 12th grade presented its own challenges. Balancing academic requirements with aerospace ambitions required strategic prioritization. The decision was made to focus on academics first, with the understanding that the aerospace work would resume when the timing was right. The rest is yet to be written.

04Departments

Our Trinity of Space Divisions

Department of Aerospace, Astronomy and Astrophysics (DOAAA)

We design, develop and build spacecrafts that takes humanity to the far extents of space and we research the unknown to understand about it.

Department of Infrastructure, Energy and Logistics (DIEL)

Since energy powers the universe, we handle the infrastructure needed to generate energy for space applications, ensure that they are functioning for the international agencies, and handle the necessary logistics.

Department of Planetary Protection and Defense (DOPPD)

Humanity is always on the edge of extinction and when any threat arises, The division will try to bring International Agencies and resources to protect ourselves and the future of mankind.
